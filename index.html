Quiz Rules & Instructions

  1. There are four Difficulty Modes available to you.
  2. Press the ‘Get Started!’ button to select your Difficulty Mode.
  3. Each Difficulty Mode has 20 Questions, each question has to be attempted before moving onto the next.
  4. Your score will be calculated at the end and feedback will be given based on your results - Good Luck!
